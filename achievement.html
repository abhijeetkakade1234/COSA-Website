2023-2024

We proudly secured the House Cup I championship for the academic year 2023-2024. This prestigious award recognizes excellence in academics, sports, and extracurricular activities. Our team demonstrated exceptional talent, determination, and teamwork throughout the year.

2024-2025

Building on our previous success, we are honored to win the House Cup II for the 2024-2025 academic year. This consecutive victory highlights our commitment to excellence and continuous improvement in all areas of competition and collaboration.

Our creative talents were recognized with the prestigious Art Gallery Award for the 2024-2025 season. This award celebrates innovation, artistic expression, and cultural contribution. Our exhibition featured diverse artworks that captivated audiences and critics alike.
